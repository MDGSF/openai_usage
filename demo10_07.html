The SWC-100 Chair is a beautiful mid-century inspired piece that is perfect for any home or business setting. It is part of a family of office furniture that includes filing cabinets, desks, bookcases, and meeting tables. You can choose between soft or hard-floor caster options and two choices of seat foam densities: medium (1.8 1b/ft3) or high (2.8 1b/ft3). The chair is also available with armless or 8 position PU armrests.

The seat is made of HD36 foam, ensuring that you will be comfortable while sitting for long periods of time. The SWC-100 Chair is made in Italy, ensuring that you are getting a high-quality product that is built to last.
